We're here for you!

The Cyber Congress of Amity-46, Gurugram is here to guide you through the realms of cyber security. We propagate netiquette and ensure adherence to Amity’s cyber laws. In case you need any help—professional or friendly—let us know.
Thanks for stopping by!
Cyber Congress is the cyber-regulatory body of Amity International School, Sector-46, Gurugram. We constantly work under the guidance of our esteemed Principal ma’am, Ms. Arti Chopra, and Cyber Security Expert, Mr. Rakshit Tandon, to execute the cyber laws of the school.

The Cyber Congress conducts regular workshops and activities—both in-person and online—to educate students and teachers about cyber-related crimes, as well as their respective preventive measures.

We have developed this platform to increase our approachability for the entire school—especially when the school is online. In case you need to contact us, please use the Report section.




We understand that speaking up against a cyber crime—especially on a public domain—can be extremely intimidating; thus, we promise to keep your information private. We also allow you to be completely anonymous to us—if you are not ready to reveal your identity yet.
